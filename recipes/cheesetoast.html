Cheese on Toast

Cheese on toast makes a delicious lunch

An absolute classic, the perfect lunch on a busy day.

The ingredients list and recipe instructions can be found below. Makes 1 portion.

Cheesey does it!

Ingredients

  • 50g extra mature cheddar
  • 2 thick slices of bread, white bloomer preferred
  • Worcestershire sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Turn on grill.
  2. Place slices of bread into toaster for 1 minute at low toasting setting.
  3. Grate the cheddar.
  4. Remove bread from toaster and place grated cheese of one side of each slice of bread.
  5. Place bread under grill, cheesey side up, until cheese is melting and bubbling.
  6. Optional: Add Worcestershire sauce for an extra kick!
