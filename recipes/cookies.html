Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate Chip Cookies are everyone's favourite

This recipe is a great one to help get kids interested in the kitchen.

The ingredients list and recipe instructions can be found below. Makes 10 cookies.

Get Baking!

Ingredients

  • 120g butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 180g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 150g chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 180C/160 fan and line a baking sheet with paper.
  2. Mix together butter and sugar, then beat in egg and vanilla.
  3. Add in flour, bicarbonate of soda, and chocolate chips.
  4. Use a tablespoon to measure out a portion onto the tray.
  5. Repeat step 4 until you have 10 evenly-spaced scoops of mixture on the tray.
  6. Bake cookies in oven for 12 minutes.
  7. Leave cookies to cool for a few minutes.
  8. Enjoy warm with a glass of milk!
